ذاتی روحانی مطالعہ
Five concepts studied in sequence — each one preparing the ground for the next. Dhikr places Allah in awareness. Zuhd clears what would distort it. Taqwa guards it. Muraqabah deepens it. Ihsan is what you do from that ground.
